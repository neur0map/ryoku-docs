**力と美のために** : *for the sake of power and beauty.*

Ryoku is a premium Arch workstation for capable desktops and laptops. It is
pre-riced, plugin-minded, and tuned for people who want a fast, polished daily
machine from first boot.

The desktop is [Hyprland](https://hypr.land/), driven by a
[Quickshell](https://quickshell.outfoxxed.me) UI: a morphing top bar (the pill),
the launcher, a control deck, the lock screen, and the Ryoku Settings control
center. Capture, updates, hardware, media, and developer tools are built into the
shell, not bolted on as tray-only scripts.

## What ships

- **Desktop**: a Hyprland session with a Quickshell pill, launcher, control deck, lock screen, and the Ryoku Settings control center.
- **The `ryoku` command**: one tool for updates, rollback, status, and self-healing, backed by signed packages and, on a btrfs root, snapper snapshots wrapped around every update.
- **Theming**: wallpaper-driven colors via wallust, plus switchable full-system themes (Ryoku Default, Catppuccin, Tokyo Night, Gruvbox, Nord, Rosé Pine, and more).
- **Daily-driver fit**: development, content work, media, notes, terminals, and container workflows on strong hardware.

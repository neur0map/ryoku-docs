**力と美のために** : *for the sake of power and beauty.*

Ryoku is a premium Arch workstation for capable desktops and laptops. It is pre-riced, plugin-minded, and tuned for people who want a fast, polished daily machine from first boot.

The desktop is [Hyprland](https://hypr.land/), with a [Quickshell](https://quickshell.outfoxxed.me)-based top bar, sidebars, lock screen, launcher, and control center. Workflow surfaces (capture, updates, hardware, media, and developer tools) are built into the shell instead of bolted on as tray-only scripts.

## What ships

- **Desktop**: a Hyprland session with a Quickshell top bar, sidebar, lock screen, launcher, and the Ryoku Settings control center.
- **The `ryoku` command**: one tool for updates, rollback, status, and self-healing, backed by signed packages and, on a btrfs root, snapper snapshots wrapped around every update.
- **Theming**: wallpaper-driven colors via wallust, plus switchable full-system themes (Greek Noir default, Catppuccin, Tokyo Night, Gruvbox, Nord, Rose Pine, and more).
- **Daily-driver fit**: development, content work, media, notes, terminals, and container workflows on strong hardware.

## How it is delivered

Ryoku ships as signed pacman packages from the `[ryoku]` repository. The installer adds that repo, installs `ryoku-desktop`, and lays your configuration down with `ryoku materialize`. Updates and config always flow from the repo to your system, never the other way around.

## Status

Public preview. ISOs are built and signed through the release pipeline; see the [Ryoku website](https://ryoku.dev/) for current availability. The shell is moving from its Omarchy inheritance toward a Ryoku-owned plugin shell, while installer, command, and runtime behavior stay coherent.
